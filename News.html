Smart Sports Solutions Co. to attend LEAP 2025 in Riyadh

February 9-12, 2025
Riyadh Exhibition Centre

Smart Sports Solutions Co., a leading Saudi-based sports technology company, is set to participate in LEAP 2025, the region's premier technology event, taking place from February 9 to 12 at the Riyadh Exhibition and Convention Centre, Malham.

LEAP, organised by the Ministry of Communication and Information Technology (MCIT) in collaboration with the Saudi Federation for Cybersecurity, Programming and Drones (SAFCSP), is a global platform bringing together pioneers in technology and innovation. The event will cover key sectors such as Fintech, Edutech, Smart Cities, AI, Health Tech, and the Fourth Industrial Revolution (4IR).

Smart Sports Solutions Co. will participate and interact with the potential entities for possible collaborations and partnership in the sports tech and AI, reinforcing its commitment to revolutionising the industry with AI-driven performance analytics and other solutions for athletes and sports organisations.

The company's participation aligns with Saudi Arabia's Vision 2030 initiative, emphasising digital transformation and innovation in sports. As part of the event, DeepFest, LEAP's dedicated AI stream powered by the Saudi Authority for Data and Artificial Intelligence (SDAIA), will highlight advancements in artificial intelligence, a key area of focus for Smart Sports Solutions Co.

For more details about the company's participation and its agenda, email us at contact@smartcaptain.ai

Smart Sports Solutions Co. to Participate in Private Sector Forum 2025

February 12-13, 2025
King Abdul Aziz International Conference Center

Smart Sports Solutions Co., a leading Saudi-based sports technology company, is set to take part in the Private Sector Forum 2025, hosted by the Public Investment Fund (PIF) on February 12-13, 2025, at the King Abdul Aziz International Conference Center in Riyadh.

The third edition of the PIF Private Sector Forum serves as a key platform to enhance collaboration between PIF, its portfolio companies, and the private sector. The event will highlight investment opportunities, showcase business prospects within PIF's ecosystem, and foster partnerships that drive economic growth in line with Saudi Vision 2030.

As a pioneer in sports technology, Smart Sports Solutions Co. will explore new avenues for collaboration, leveraging its expertise in AI-driven sports analytics, smart infrastructure solutions, and digital transformation. The company's participation underscores its commitment to fostering innovation and contributing to the Kingdom's evolving sports landscape.

For more information about Smart Sports Solutions Co.'s role in the Private Sector Forum 2025, email us at contact@smartcaptain.ai
