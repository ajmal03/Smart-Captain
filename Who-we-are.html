Who we are

Imagine a world where every athlete’s true potential is unlocked, where teams train smarter, compete harder, and federations make data-driven decisions with absolute confidence. This is the future we are building at Smart Sports Solutions. We don’t just follow innovation—we lead it, using AI, machine learning, and AR/VR-powered technology to transform how sports are played, coached, and managed. Inspired by Saudi Vision 2030, our mission is clear: to create a thriving sports ecosystem that nurtures talent, enhances performance, and redefines excellence.

With over a decade of experience, our roots in Saudi Arabia have grown into a global force in sports innovation. We have worked alongside athletes, coaches, and organisations worldwide, proving that the right blend of technology and vision can turn potential into podium finishes. From grassroots development to elite competition, we provide the solutions that make the difference between good and great—between participation and domination.
